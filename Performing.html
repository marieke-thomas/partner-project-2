Performing

I love performing (which explains why I also enjoy teaching). Here are a few of the performing groups that I've been a part of!

Gotham Rock Choir

I'm a current member of the Gotham Rock Choir, which is a community choir dedicated to singing Rock, Pop, and Soul music. Here we are performing the songs "Price Tag" and "You Will Be Found".

Harvard Noteables

In college, I was a member and choreographer for the Harvard Noteables, a non-audition Broadway show choir. Here is me singing "One-Feathered Tail" from Seussical, and here's a more lyrical duet. One of my favorite songs to choreograph was "Hynos: Evoe!" from The Frogs.

Harvard Pops Orchestra

I was also a cello player and Creative Board member of the Harvard Pops Orchestra. Here I am playing as an alum in the 20th Anniversary Concert!
