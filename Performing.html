Performing

I love performing (which explains why I also enjoy teaching). Here are a few of the performing groups that I've been a part of!

Gotham Rock Choir

I'm a current member of the Gotham Rock Choir, which is a community choir dedicated to singing Rock, Pop, and Soul music.

Harvard Noteables

In college, I was a member and choreographer for the Harvard Noteables, a non-audition Broadway show choir. Here's me singing two of my favorite songs, one very silly ("One Feathered Tail," from Seussical the Musical), and one a more serious lyrical duet ("Who Will Love Me As I Am?" from Sideshow).

I was a Production Manager for the Noteables for two years, during which I choreographed group numbers for four concerts. One of my favorite songs to choreograph was "Hymnos: Evoe!" from The Frogs.

Harvard Pops Orchestra

I was also a cello player and Creative Board member of the Harvard Pops Orchestra. Here I am playing as an alum in the 20th Anniversary Concert!
